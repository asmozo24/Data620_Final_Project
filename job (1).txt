A Data Analyst is a professional who develops physical data models at the application system level based upon collects and analyzes data across the business to make informed decisions through a solid integrated data architectural foundation to drive informed decisions.    Responsibilities include but not limited to:  Developing physical data models at the application system level in alignment with logical business data architecture.  Evaluating existing data systems' databases and data requirements to perform data fit gap analysis and risk assessments.  Analyzing and translating business needs into long-term solution physical data models.  Reviewing modifications of existing application systems for integration across the enterprise.  Interpreting data and analyzing results using statistical techniques and providing report out on findings.  Evaluating implemented data systems for variances, discrepancies, and efficiency.  Identify, analyze, and interpret trends or patterns in complex data sets.  Performing root cause analysis to investigate data quality concerns.  Performing physical data archeology and data exploration.  Deliver solutions with an enterprise design and mindset (using sound data architecture, improving data quality, and creating robust solutions).  Supporting data governance (business term definition, data models, all things data).  Developing SIPOC diagrams (Supplier Input Process Output Consumer) for application system.  Provide expertise on database management/design to drive integration of business data, functions, and application systems.  Involvement in establishing standards, guidelines, procedures, and other infrastructure necessary to support the objective of integration of data.  Work closely with Data Architects to implement data modeling solutions in order to streamline and support enterprise information management.  Design models that are enterprise oriented, reduce data redundancy, streamline data movements, and improve enterprise information management.  Conducting full data lifecycle analysis focusing on data requirements, activities and design.  Design and implement physical data modeling solutions using relational, dimensional, and NoSQL databases.  Monitor performance and quality control plans to identify improvements.
